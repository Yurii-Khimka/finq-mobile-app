How it works

Three small steps. One big shift.

01 — Income

Money arrives, money splits.

Add an income and finQ instantly distributes it across your four envelopes. No mental math, no decision fatigue.

02 — Spend

Expenses cascade.

Buy groceries — Mandatory takes the hit. Buy a concert ticket — Optional does. If one runs dry, the next quietly covers it, and you'll see exactly why.

03 — Reflect

Patterns surface gently.

A simple heatmap shows when you spend most. Daily Budget tells you what's safe. No charts that demand action — just clarity that earns trust.
