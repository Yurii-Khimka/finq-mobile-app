1. Overview

finQ ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal finance companion designed to help you build healthy money habits. We believe your financial data is private by default, and this policy explains exactly what we do — and don't — touch.

By using finQ, you agree to the practices described below. If anything is unclear, please reach out.

2. What we collect

finQ is structured to keep your financial data on your device whenever possible. The categories of data we may handle are:

  • Account info. Name and email, only if you create an account or contact support.
  • Financial entries. Incomes, expenses, envelopes, and goals you add inside the app — stored locally.
  • Diagnostic data. Anonymous crash logs and basic usage events to keep the app stable. No transaction details.
  • Communications. Anything you send us via the contact form or email.

We do not link to your bank, scrape your statements, or read your SMS.

3. How we use it

We use the data described above only to:

  • Run the features you explicitly use (e.g. computing the discipline waterfall, daily budget, audit summary).
  • Reply to your messages and support requests.
  • Improve stability via aggregated, anonymous diagnostics — opt-out at any time in Settings.

We never sell your data, and we never use it to train marketing or advertising profiles.

4. Sharing

We don't share your data with third parties for advertising or analytics resale. The only narrow cases in which limited information may be shared:

  • Infrastructure providers we use to deliver the app (e.g. crash reporting, email delivery for support replies). They process data on our instructions only.
  • Legal compliance when required by valid law enforcement requests in the relevant jurisdiction.

5. Storage & security

Financial entries are stored on your device. Account and support data are stored on encrypted servers in the EU. We use industry-standard transport encryption (TLS) and at-rest encryption for any data we host on your behalf.

No system is perfectly secure, but we work to minimize the data we hold so that there is little to compromise in the first place.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data.
  • Correct or update inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for diagnostic collection.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

To exercise any of these, email privacy@finq.app. We aim to respond within 14 days.

7. Children

finQ is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. When changes are material, we'll notify you via the app or email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current revision.

9. Contact

Questions, concerns, or curiosities? Email privacy@finq.app or use the feedback form. We genuinely read every message.
